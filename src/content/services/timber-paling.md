Timber paling fences are the classic Australian backyard fence. They offer privacy, a natural look, and can be customised with capping, lattice, or stained finishes.

## What is included

- Treated pine or hardwood pales and rails
- Standard 1.8m or 2.1m heights available
- Plinth boards for ground clearance and rot protection
- Optional timber capping, lattice extensions, or stain finish
- Full site clean-up and old fence removal

## Best fit

Best for homeowners who want an affordable, natural-looking fence. Great for established gardens, period homes, and anyone who prefers the warmth of timber over steel.
