Living in the Yarra Valley often means larger blocks, horses, or livestock. We build rural fences that stand up to weather, animals, and time — using quality treated pine posts, heavy-gauge wire, and proven construction methods.

## What is included

- Treated pine or hardwood posts, strainers, and stays
- Post-and-wire (ringlock, hinge-joint, or barbed wire)
- Post-and-rail for driveways and visible boundary lines
- Horse-safe mesh and electric fencing options
- Gate installations for paddock and property access

## Best fit

Acreage and lifestyle property owners, horse agistment, small farms, and anyone in the Yarra Valley or Dandenong Ranges who needs a sturdy rural fence built by locals who understand the area.
